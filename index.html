About Us

Tsukikage is a multi-sector investment holding with a diversified portfolio, led by a new generation of technologists and financiers. Our strategic growth includes expansion into Asian and European markets. With a strong focus on scientific innovation, sustainable development, and digital transformation, we invest in ventures that shape the future. Our approach blends capital, technology, and deep expertise to support scalable, meaningful businesses across key industries. We thrive at the intersection of progress, purpose, and global ambition.
Our Story
Founded on the belief that innovation drives long-term value, our company began as a visionary alliance between digital architects and financial experts. From early investments in emerging technologies to expanding into logistics, pharmaceutics, and real estate, we’ve built a legacy of growth and resilience. Each milestone reflects our drive to empower industries with smart capital and sustainable solutions. As Tsukikage grows, we continue to prioritize purpose, partnerships, and people — building a diversified ecosystem for the future.
Our Mission
Our mission is to empower transformative ventures through strategic investment and innovation. We aim to support companies that tackle real-world challenges with scalable, sustainable solutions. Whether advancing pharmaceutical research or driving digital infrastructure, we believe in purposeful progress. We are committed to enabling long-term value creation, responsible growth, and global impact. Every decision we make is guided by our dedication to technology, scientific excellence, and ethical investment principles that shape a smarter, better-connected world.
